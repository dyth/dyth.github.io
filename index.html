Research Interests

I want to create robots that understand language. Through language, people can inform, direct and give feedback to robots in everyday settings.

    Long Term: the Wozniak test; instruction-following; grounded language learning

    Short-Medium Term: energy-based models; targeted exploration; hypernetworks; compositional generalisation
